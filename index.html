Is Super Simple Contacts an Apple Contacts alternative?

Yes. It is a native contact manager and address book for Mac, iPhone, and iPad that works with Apple Contacts while adding faster search, smart groups, duplicate review, safe sync, and complete editing tools.

Can I sync Google and Microsoft contacts with my Mac and iPhone?

Yes. Connect multiple Google Contacts accounts and Microsoft personal or work accounts alongside Apple Contacts. Account ownership stays visible, and you can review outgoing changes before sync.

Does the contacts app work on macOS, iOS, and iPadOS?

Yes. One subscription includes native Super Simple Contacts experiences for Mac, iPhone, and iPad, with layouts designed for each Apple device.

Can I search contacts from Raycast or Alfred?

Yes. Optional Raycast and Alfred integrations search the same private local index. Find a person, copy a detail, start an email or call, and open a contact without leaving your Mac launcher.
